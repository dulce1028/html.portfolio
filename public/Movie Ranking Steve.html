The Best Movies According to Steve

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Forest Gump

I loved this story and the way it was told. The CGI was incredible as well.

District 9

This low budget Sci-Fi movie had a relatively unknown cast but was extremly well done. Loved the aliens and the storyline!

American Psycho

A comedy-horror staring Christian Bale, it had both great dialogue and grusome scenes
